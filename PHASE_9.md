# Phase 9 — Resume Builder Summary

## Achievements
- **Integrated Resume Editor**: Developed the `/resume` page as a dynamic editor, allowing users to pre-fill content from their GitHub profile and AI summaries, and then manually edit.
- **Template System**: Implemented a template switcher with three distinct styles:
    - **Modern**: A visually rich, contemporary layout.
    - **Minimal**: A clean, streamlined presentation.
    - **ATS Friendly**: Optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems.
- **Real-time Preview**: Users can see their resume update in real-time as they edit and switch templates.
- **PDF Export**: Integrated browser-native print functionality for seamless PDF generation and printing.
- **Data Persistence**: Ensured resume content and template preferences are saved via the `/api/user/resume` endpoint, utilizing the `ResumeProfile` Prisma model.
